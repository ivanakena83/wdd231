Kampala Football Clubs Association | Site Plan

Site Name

Kampala Football Clubs Association

This name identifies the organization as a hub for football (soccer) clubs in Kampala, Uganda.

Optional domain: kampalafootballclubs.org

Site Purpose

The website provides a central platform for football clubs in Kampala to connect, share news, events, league schedules, membership information, and resources for players, coaches, and fans.

Scenarios

  • Which clubs are participating in the upcoming Kampala football league?
  • How can I register my team to join the association?

Color Scheme

  • Dark Blue (#004aad): Headings, footer background, and accents.
  • Light Blue (#e6f2ff): Section backgrounds and highlights.

Typography

  • Roboto, sans-serif: Used for body text and paragraphs.
  • Roboto Bold: Used for headings and titles.
