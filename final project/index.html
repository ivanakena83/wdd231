About KFCA

Established in 2015, we bring together clubs from all divisions to foster collaboration and growth.

Our Mission

To organize competitive leagues, provide coaching resources, and support youth development.

Our Vision

To see Kampala become a hub of football excellence with thriving clubs and passionate fans.
