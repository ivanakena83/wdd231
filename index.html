Welcome to the Gulu Chamber of Commerce

Our Mission

The Gulu Chamber of Commerce is dedicated to supporting local businesses, promoting economic growth, and fostering a strong business community in Gulu City and the surrounding region.

Why Join the Chamber?

Members of the Gulu Chamber of Commerce gain access to networking opportunities, business promotion, professional training, and advocacy that strengthens the local economy.
